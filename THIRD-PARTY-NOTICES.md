# Third-Party Notices

## EFF wordlists

Hoststamp bundles these Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF) wordlists:

- `eff_short_wordlist_1.txt`, EFF Short Wordlist #1, 1,296 entries
- `eff_short_wordlist_2_0.txt`, EFF Short Wordlist #2, 1,296 entries
- `eff_large_wordlist.txt`, EFF Long Wordlist, 7,776 entries

Notes:

- EFF's 2016 long wordlist announcement describes the long list as using words
  between 3 and 9 characters.
- EFF Short Wordlist #1 is a separate four-dice list featuring only short
  words.
- EFF Short Wordlist #2 is a separate four-dice list featuring longer words
  that may be more memorable, including 10-character words.
